Traffic Light

Modular system that allows an efficient and systematized installation of smart traffic lights, luminaire, and signage in public spaces. Depending on the length of the aluminum profile used, it can be adapted to different configurations, such as traffic lights, LED lighting, tourist information, electronic signage in real-time for tolls, bus stops, or trains.
